Profile

India is one of the oldest civilizations in the world with a kaleidoscopic variety and rich cultural heritage.

The design of the National Flag was adopted by the Constituent Assembly of India on 22 July 1947.

Bounded by the Great Himalayas in the north, it stretches southwards and at the Tropic of Cancer, tapers off into the Indian Ocean between the Bay of Bengal on the east and the Arabian Sea on the west.
